State of Play

Client: Visit California
Role: Designer & Developer 

Three California mommy bloggers share their region’s most family-friendly destinations as the client attempts to capitalize on the summer months to increase tourism across the state.

To convey a roadtrip’s continuous journey, we paired destinations’ still imagery with a dynamic, static map illustration of the route being described.

The site contained 15 discrete pieces of content in one long scroll, so to reduce the overall footprint for the user, we created expandable slideshows to only display content the user was intrigued by.
